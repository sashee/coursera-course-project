Tamas Sallai

Want to play a game?

Great!

This game is called Guess the slope, as your job is to identify the slope of a regression line for randomly generated points.

You can play the game at https://sashee.shinyapps.io/coursera-course-project .

Your progress are tracked during your session, so you can try to beat your best every time you play!

How to play

Use the slider to adjust the line until you see fit.

Submitting

If you think the slope is set to the correct value, press Submit, and you'll instantly know whether you are right or not.

Have fun!
